ABOUT CANARIES

It is no coincidence that the Canary Islands are called European Hawaii - this is the perfect place for surfing fans: stable warm wind all year round, sandy beaches, kilometers of pristine expanses and clear sea. And Fuerteventura, the second-largest island in the archipelago, becomes a real mecca for surfers.

It is here that the best place in Spain to be trained in surfing. In addition, in winter and huge Atlantic waves come here in autumn, Fuerteventura boasts many shallow calm lagoons, great for training beginner surfers.

Surfing in the Canary Islands is available year-round - and waves and the right weather is always there. And a wave suitable for the level will be able to find both a real pro and a beginner. It is no coincidence that it is believed that this is the place is best suited for learning here, it is possible to move to a new level without difficulties.

Listing the advantages, one cannot get around the fact that the beaches of Fuerteventura are the longest in the Canaries. In the end, it is impossible to imagine them overflowing with tourists. You will face only those who, like you, will be one goal to be interested in is learning to surf.

So far, you only want to understand what surfing is, or have already experienced the beauty of wave riding, but want to raise your level? Our surfing school will be able to offer training for any level of training.

Four hours of exciting activities a day will help you confidently conquer the waves, feel real surfer.

The main advantages of our Russian school are the high quality of teaching and organizing classes, security (all our customers are insured with Allianz), excellent inventory and equipment (provided for all training course), including soft safety boards for beginners.

First, we determine the level of skating and physical fitness and only then draw up a program preparation. And we can assure that after the training ends, you will not only receive a mass great sensations, but this level will rise to a new step.
